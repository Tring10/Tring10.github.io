About Tring

Tring is a project deicated to home safety.
Itcan be mounted on your door or the wall beside your door.
it detect the faces of the person standing in front of it and then close, open or lock the door by itself, if the photo of the face of a person is stored in the memory of the device and it recognizes the face ,then the door opens and closes but if the face is not recognized and it senses some weird movement on the area it recognizes it as an intruder and then locks the door.
It also Provides Keypad and BLUETOOTH features to smartly open your door .

We are currently testing all the features that it can provide and also improving them.
To be clear this project is in the middle of ideating and building stage

It can be a great device for houses where there are small kids who cant open the door on their own or for areas where there a lot of robbery is happening
